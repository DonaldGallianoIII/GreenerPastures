Model: egg laying is a Poisson process at swarm × rate eggs/hr; each egg is shiny at the tier odds. Time to N shinies uses the Erlang/Poisson CDF (not just mean × N). Reality check: Crystal needs a shiny parent in every pasture and Combo needs two — a 50-pasture Crystal swarm wants 50 shiny parents (or shiny Dittos). Most real orders run pure Masuda; treat Crystal/Combo as "what a shiny parent would buy." Defaults: base 1/8192, Masuda 1/2048, Crystal 1/512, Combo 1/128.
