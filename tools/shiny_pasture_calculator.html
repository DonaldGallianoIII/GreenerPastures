Mixed tiers: a species can hold regular (Masuda) and shiny-parent (Crystal) pastures at once — its Eff. odds is the blended per-egg rate across that mix, and its bar shows the tier split inside the species' share.
Odds: base × Masuda × Crystal(shiny parents), multiplicative (defaults: Masuda 1/2048 · +Crystal 1/512 · +2 1/128). One shiny Eevee parent among regulars pulls that line's blended odds up sharply even at a single pasture. Waits use 1 − e−λt.
